WHL-VA-AC-07

Vehicle Delivery Coordination and Driver Assignment

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process coordinates the delivery of newly acquired vehicles to fleet clients and assigns drivers to those vehicles. It ensures seamless integration into the fleet management system and compliance with all regulatory requirements.
Trigger
Receipt of a purchase order from a client for new vehicles
Outcome
Successful delivery of vehicles to the client's location, assignment of drivers, and updates in the FleetView VIM and SAP S/4HANA systems

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • OBD device installation success rate > 95%

  • Handover confirmation time < 1 hour
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delays in order processing due to manual data entry errors
  • Delays in procurement due to outdated vendor contracts
  • Inaccurate delivery dates due to unexpected traffic
  • Device malfunctions due to incompatible software versions
  • Incorrect asset records affecting financial reporting
