WHL-VA-AC-08

New Vehicle Title and Registration Processing

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves the acquisition and ordering of new vehicles, including title and registration processing at Wheels.
Trigger
Receipt of a purchase order for a new vehicle from a client via FleetView.
Outcome
Successful completion produces a fully titled and registered vehicle ready for deployment to a client.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWheels Registration Services

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Order creation time < 1 hour
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Asset update time < 2 hours
  • Final invoice generation time < 1 hour
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delayed order completions affecting client timelines.
  • Errors in final invoices leading to client disputes.
  • Inaccurate asset accounting records causing financial discrepancies.
