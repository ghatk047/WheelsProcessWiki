WHL-VA-AC-01

Fleet Policy Review and Vehicle Selector Consultation

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Fleet Policy Review and Vehicle Selector Consultation process involves reviewing fleet policies with clients and selecting appropriate vehicles based on client needs and company standards. This ensures compliance and optimizes the vehicle acquisition process.
Trigger
Client request for new or replacement vehicles
Outcome
A detailed vehicle selection report and policy compliance checklist are provided to the client, initiating the vehicle ordering process.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle selection accuracy > 95%
  • Cost accuracy > 97%
  • Client approval rate > 90%
  • Order processing time < 3 days
  • On-time policy reviews > 95%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Miscommunication leading to incorrect order creation.
  • Budget miscalculations requiring manual corrections.
  • Outdated data causing inaccurate vehicle recommendations.
  • Missing information delaying client approvals.
  • System integration issues impacting order processing times.
