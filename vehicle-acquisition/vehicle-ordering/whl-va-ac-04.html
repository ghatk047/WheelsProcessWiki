WHL-VA-AC-04

Dealer Allocation and Stock Procurement

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 7 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Dealer Allocation and Stock Procurement process involves identifying suitable dealerships to fulfill vehicle acquisition orders based on specific criteria such as location, inventory availability, and dealer performance. This ensures timely delivery of vehicles while maintaining high standards for inventory management.
Trigger
Initiation of a vehicle acquisition order through the FleetView VIM system by a Fleet Consultant or Account Manager.
Outcome
Successful allocation of dealerships to fulfill vehicle orders, ensuring that all procurement needs are met with minimal delays and optimal cost-efficiency.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Order creation time < 1 hour
  • Budget approval time < 2 hours
  • Dealer identification time < 3 hours
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate order entry leading to procurement delays.
  • Misalignment between budget and procurement needs.
  • Limited dealer options affecting timely vehicle delivery.
  • Communication breakdown between account managers and dealers.
  • Delayed status updates impacting visibility into procurement progress.
