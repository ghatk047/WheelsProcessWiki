WHL-VA-AC-06

Manufacturer Incentive and Fleet Discount Management

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the application and tracking of manufacturer incentives and fleet discounts during vehicle acquisition for Wheels. It ensures that all eligible benefits are captured and applied accurately to optimize costs and maintain compliance.
Trigger
Initiation by a Fleet Consultant or Account Manager upon receipt of a new vehicle order request from a client.
Outcome
Successful application of manufacturer incentives and fleet discounts, leading to optimized acquisition cost and accurate invoicing.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle acquisition cost optimization > 10%
  • Order accuracy > 98%
  • Confirmation time < 2 hours
  • Incentive lookup and application accuracy > 95%
  • Discount application accuracy > 97%
  • Final review accuracy > 95%
  • Invoice generation time < 3 hours
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Misidentification or missed manufacturer incentives.
  • Incorrect financial reservations leading to over/under budgeting.
  • Errors in discount application affecting overall fleet cost management.
  • Delayed order confirmation impacting acquisition timelines.
  • Final review errors requiring rework.
