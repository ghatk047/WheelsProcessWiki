WHL-VA-AC-05

Vehicle Specification and Fleet Policy Enforcement

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 10 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Vehicle Specification and Fleet Policy Enforcement process ensures that all vehicle acquisitions comply with fleet policies, specifications, and regulatory requirements. It involves the creation of detailed vehicle orders, validation against company policies, and tracking of order status.
Trigger
Initiated by a new vehicle acquisition request from an account manager or fleet consultant based on client needs and policy guidelines.
Outcome
Successful completion produces a fully specified and approved vehicle order that aligns with corporate policies and is ready for procurement and delivery.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Order creation time < 2 hours
  • Policy validation accuracy > 98%
  • Compatibility check accuracy > 95%
  • Plan update time < 30 minutes
  • Notification delivery time < 5 minutes
  • Order review accuracy > 97%
  • Invoice posting time < 10 minutes
  • Report generation time < 10 minutes
  • Order closure time < 5 minutes
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Policy changes not reflecting in the validation system.
  • WEX fuel card network issues leading to compatibility problems.
  • Data inconsistencies between SAP S/4HANA and FleetView systems.
  • Notification failures causing communication breakdowns.
  • Invoice posting errors due to system malfunctions.
