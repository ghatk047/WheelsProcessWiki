WHL-VA-AC-02

OEM Factory Order Placement and Tracking

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Acquisition & Ordering 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves placing and tracking OEM factory orders for vehicle acquisition at Wheels. It includes order creation, approval, placement, and ongoing status updates.
Trigger
Receipt of a signed purchase order (PO) from the client via FleetView or direct communication.
Outcome
Successful order placement with continuous monitoring until delivery and receipt confirmation by the client.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Order creation time < 24 hours
  • Approval time < 48 hours
  • Device configuration time < 48 hours
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • OEM integration failures leading to delayed order placement.
  • Manual approval delays affecting overall acquisition timeline.
  • Device setup errors impacting initial vehicle operations and safety.
