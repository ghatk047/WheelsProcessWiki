WHL-VA-UP-02

Upfitter Vendor Selection and Purchase Order Issuance

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves selecting an upfitter vendor and issuing a purchase order for vehicle upfitting at Wheels.
Trigger
Receiving a request from the Fleet Consultant or Account Manager for vehicle upfitting needs based on client requirements.
Outcome
A purchase order is issued to the selected upfitter vendor, detailing the specifications and costs of the upfitting work.
