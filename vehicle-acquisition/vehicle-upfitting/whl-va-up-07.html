WHL-VA-UP-07

Vehicle Delivery to Driver after Upfitting

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves delivering a vehicle to a driver after it has been upfitted and configured according to the client's specifications. The process ensures that all necessary documentation is completed and the vehicle is in perfect condition for the driver to use.
Trigger
Completion of vehicle upfitting and configuration as confirmed by the Maintenance Coordinator
Outcome
Vehicle is delivered to the designated driver with all required documents and systems operational

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Document completeness > 95%
  • Fuel card setup time < 24 hours
  • Telematics setup time < 1 hour
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Incorrect upfitting details leading to rework and delays
  • Billing errors causing financial discrepancies with clients
  • Missing or incorrect documentation delaying vehicle delivery
  • GPS configuration issues affecting telematics data collection
  • Vehicle not meeting client specifications requiring additional upfitting
