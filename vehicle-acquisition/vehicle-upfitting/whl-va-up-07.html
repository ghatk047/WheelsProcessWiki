WHL-VA-UP-07

Vehicle Delivery to Driver after Upfitting

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the delivery of a vehicle to a driver after it has been upfitted and configured. It ensures that all necessary documents are prepared, the vehicle is properly registered, and the driver receives the vehicle with all required materials.
Trigger
Completion of vehicle upfitting and configuration by Upfitting Team
Outcome
Vehicle is delivered to the designated driver with all upfitting items installed and operational. All relevant documentation and systems are updated.
