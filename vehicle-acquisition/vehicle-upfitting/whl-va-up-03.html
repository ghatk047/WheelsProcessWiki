WHL-VA-UP-03

Chassis-to-Upfitter Transportation Coordination

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Chassis-to-Upfitter Transportation Coordination process ensures seamless transportation of chassis from the manufacturer to the designated upfitter for customization and configuration according to client specifications.
Trigger
Receipt of a fully built chassis by Wheels' logistics team after manufacturing completion.
Outcome
Chassis safely transported to the upfitter with all necessary documentation and tracking details updated in FleetView and SAP S/4HANA systems.

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate chassis order details leading to delivery delays.
  • Synchronization issues between FleetView and SAP S/4HANA causing data discrepancies.
  • Delayed shipment reports impacting logistics planning.
  • Lack of real-time tracking for chassis shipments.
  • Manual entry errors in chassis delivery status updates.
