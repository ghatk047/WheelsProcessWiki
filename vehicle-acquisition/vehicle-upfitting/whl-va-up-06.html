WHL-VA-UP-06

Upfit Cost Tracking and Invoice Processing

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process tracks costs associated with vehicle upfitting and processes invoices to ensure accurate billing.
Trigger
Vehicle order completion in FleetView VIM
Outcome
Accurate cost tracking and processed invoices for upfitted vehicles.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANA

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data mismatch between FleetView and SAP leading to inaccurate billing.
  • System downtime impacting cost tracking and invoice processing.
  • Human error during configuration or data entry causing discrepancies.
  • Vendor delays in providing upfit specifications affecting timeline.
  • Payment errors due to incorrect account details.
