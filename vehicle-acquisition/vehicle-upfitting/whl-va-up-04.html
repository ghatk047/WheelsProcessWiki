WHL-VA-UP-04

Upfitting Work Order Management and Quality Inspection

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 10 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the creation and monitoring of upfitting work orders for vehicles, ensuring that all necessary components are installed correctly and efficiently.
Trigger
Receipt of a detailed upfitting request from a client specifying required modifications and configurations for a vehicle.
Outcome
Completion of upfitting tasks with verified quality assurance checks, resulting in a fully configured vehicle ready for deployment.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Work order creation time < 24 hours
  • Component availability within 72 hours
  • Upfitting progress visibility > 95%
  • Task completion accuracy > 98%
  • Pre-check completion rate > 90%
  • Inspection pass rate > 95%
  • Invoice generation time < 24 hours
  • Client notification response rate > 95%
  • Work order closure rate > 98%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delays in receiving client upfitting requests can lead to missed deadlines.
  • Stockouts or long lead times for components can delay the entire process.
  • Scheduling conflicts with other maintenance tasks can cause delays and rework.
  • Interruptions in telematics connectivity during tracking can affect visibility.
  • Manual updates leading to errors can compromise task accuracy and progress tracking.
  • Inconsistent pre-check protocols can result in unsafe deployments.
  • Non-standard components causing inspection failures can lead to additional work and delays.
