WHL-VA-UP-05

Post-Upfit Vehicle PDI and Compliance Check

🏢 Vehicle Acquisition & Upfitting ➡ Vehicle Upfitting & Configuration 📝 10 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process ensures that newly upfitted vehicles are properly prepared and inspected before being dispatched to clients, focusing on pre-delivery inspection (PDI) and compliance checks.
Trigger
Upfitting completion notification from the Upfit Coordinator
Outcome
Vehicle is ready for delivery with all required PDI and compliance checks completed
