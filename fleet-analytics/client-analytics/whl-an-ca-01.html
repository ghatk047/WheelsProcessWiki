WHL-AN-CA-01

Complex Fleet Analysis Project Scoping

🏢 Fleet Analytics & Reporting ➡ Client Analytics and Consulting 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves scoping a complex fleet analysis project for clients, utilizing FleetView and SAP S/4HANA to gather and analyze data. The goal is to deliver actionable insights that improve client operations.
Trigger
Client request for advanced fleet analytics or a strategic review of fleet performance
Outcome
A detailed scope document outlining the project's objectives, required resources, timelines, and cost estimates, ready for client approval
