WHL-FM-ON-04

Salesforce Account and Case Setup

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process outlines the steps for setting up a Salesforce account and case management system for new client onboarding in fleet management services at Wheels.
Trigger
Initiation of client onboarding process by the Account Manager after contract signing.
Outcome
A fully configured Salesforce account with necessary case management capabilities set up for the new client, enabling smooth operation and support.
