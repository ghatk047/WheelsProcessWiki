WHL-FM-ON-02

Fleet Policy Design and Driver Eligibility Rules

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves designing fleet policies and setting up driver eligibility rules for new clients at Wheels.
Trigger
A client signs an agreement with Wheels to onboard their fleet management services.
Outcome
Fleet policy documents are created, and driver eligibility rules are established, enabling the client's operations within Wheels' systems.
