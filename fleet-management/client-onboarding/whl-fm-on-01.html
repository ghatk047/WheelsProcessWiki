WHL-FM-ON-01

New Client Kickoff and Requirements Discovery

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 12 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The New Client Kickoff and Requirements Discovery process involves initial client engagement, gathering detailed requirements, and setting up the necessary configurations to onboard a new fleet management client. This ensures a seamless transition into Wheels' comprehensive fleet management solutions.
Trigger
Receiving a signed contract or a formal request for onboarding from a new client
Outcome
A fully configured Wheels account with all required services, system integrations, and custom reports tailored to the client's needs. Successful completion results in the client being ready to utilize Wheels' fleet management tools.
