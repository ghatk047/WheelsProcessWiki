WHL-FM-ON-03

FleetView Portal Configuration and User Provisioning

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves configuring the FleetView portal and provisioning users for new clients in the fleet management services domain.
Trigger
New client onboarding initiation by Account Manager
Outcome
A fully configured FleetView portal with provisioned users ready for client use
FleetViewSAP S/4HANA

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • User provisioning completion within 3 business days
  • Portal configuration within 3 business days
  • Customization implementation within 5 business days
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate client role definitions leading to access control issues
  • Misconfigured portal causing functional disruptions
  • Delays in access request processing due to system bottlenecks
  • Complex customizations requiring additional time for development and testing
  • Technical errors during user provisioning affecting initial client login
