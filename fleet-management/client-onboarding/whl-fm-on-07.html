WHL-FM-ON-07

SLA Definition and KPI Baseline Setting

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process defines Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and sets Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for new client onboarding in fleet management services at Wheels. The goal is to ensure that all SLA terms are clearly articulated and KPI baselines are established to measure operational performance.
Trigger
New client contract signed
Outcome
SLAs documented, KPIs set, and communicated to the client
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWEX Fuel Card API

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Effective communication rate > 95%
  • Deployment success rate > 98%
  • Onboarding completion rate > 90%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inconsistent SLA templates leading to client dissatisfaction
  • Manual input errors causing delays in ERP configuration
  • Failed API integrations between FleetView and WEX for fuel transactions
  • Incorrect invoices generated by SAP S/4HANA causing financial discrepancies
  • Unsuccessful onboarding meetings with clients leading to dissatisfaction
