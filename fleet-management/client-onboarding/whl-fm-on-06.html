WHL-FM-ON-06

Driver Communication and Programme Launch

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Onboarding & Programme Setup 📝 7 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process outlines the steps for setting up driver communication and programme launch at Wheels, ensuring that all necessary systems are configured and communicated to drivers effectively.
Trigger
Completion of client onboarding and setup of fleet management services
Outcome
Successful launch of the driver communication programme with all systems operational and drivers informed
