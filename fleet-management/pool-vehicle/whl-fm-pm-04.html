WHL-FM-PM-04

Pool Vehicle Utilisation Monitoring and Reporting

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Pool Vehicle & Shared Mobility Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process ensures that pool vehicle utilization is monitored and reported accurately to optimize fleet management and improve client satisfaction.
Trigger
Daily scheduled report generation or real-time alert from the Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform
Outcome
Produce a comprehensive utilization report with metrics, insights, and actionable recommendations for improving vehicle efficiency.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAInformaticaTableauKubernetes on AWS EKSOutSystems Platform

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Report generation time < 30 minutes
  • Report approval time < 24 hours
  • Financial update accuracy > 98%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data loss or corruption during data collection and integration
  • Inaccurate report generation due to data mapping errors
  • Slow deployment of analytics portal updates leading to outdated reports
  • Delays in client communication due to email delivery failures
  • Errors in financial tracking resulting from incorrect data entry
