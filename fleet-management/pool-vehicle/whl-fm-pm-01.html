WHL-FM-PM-01

Pool Vehicle Fleet Sizing and Location Planning

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Pool Vehicle & Shared Mobility Management 📝 7 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Pool Vehicle Fleet Sizing and Location Planning process involves determining the optimal number of pool vehicles needed for each location based on demand analysis and ensuring that these vehicles are strategically placed to meet client requirements efficiently.
Trigger
Client request or internal review indicating a need for pool vehicle fleet adjustments
Outcome
An optimized pool vehicle fleet with clear location assignments, ensuring efficient utilization and compliance with operational standards.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Data extraction accuracy > 98%
  • Validation accuracy > 95%
  • Configuration update time < 1 day
  • Plan finalization time < 1 day
  • Notification response time < 2 days
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate demand analysis leading to over-provisioning or under-provisioning of vehicles.
  • Errors in vehicle configuration updates causing operational disruptions.
  • Communication delays with stakeholders leading to delayed implementation.
  • Data inconsistencies between systems affecting the accuracy of fleet sizing decisions.
  • Delays in initiating the fleet sizing process due to internal processes.
