WHL-FM-PM-08

Cost Allocation and Departmental Chargeback

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Pool Vehicle & Shared Mobility Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the cost allocation and departmental chargeback for pool vehicles and shared mobility services at Wheels. It ensures accurate billing and resource allocation across different departments.
Trigger
Initiated when a vehicle is assigned to a new department or when there is a change in service usage (e.g., additional miles, extended rental period).
Outcome
Successful completion produces accurate cost allocation records and chargebacks to respective departments, ensuring fair billing and resource management.

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate vehicle reassignments leading to misallocation.
  • Complex SAP configurations causing delays in chargeback processing.
  • Data inconsistencies resulting in incorrect usage reports.
  • Handling exceptions and disputes manually slowing down the process.
  • Approval bottlenecks delaying the final closure of processes.
