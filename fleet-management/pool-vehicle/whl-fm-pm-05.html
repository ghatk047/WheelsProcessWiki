WHL-FM-PM-05

Pool Vehicle Maintenance Scheduling

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Pool Vehicle & Shared Mobility Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Pool Vehicle Maintenance Scheduling process at Wheels is responsible for ensuring that maintenance tasks are efficiently scheduled and managed to minimize downtime and maximize vehicle uptime. This process involves collaboration between various roles including Fleet Consultants, Maintenance Coordinators, and SAP Finance Analysts across systems like FleetView and SAP S/4HANA.
Trigger
An alert or notification in FleetView indicating a scheduled maintenance due date or an unexpected issue detected via the Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform.
Outcome
A successful completion of this process results in timely maintenance scheduling, accurate invoicing, and updated records in both FleetView and SAP S/4HANA. This ensures that vehicles are maintained according to their schedules, reducing downtime and enhancing fleet reliability.

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data synchronization issues between FleetView and SAP S/4HANA
  • Push notification failures leading to missed reminders or notifications
  • Billing errors due to incorrect data input during invoice generation
  • Delayed payments due to incorrect account details
  • Manual data entry errors leading to inaccurate records
