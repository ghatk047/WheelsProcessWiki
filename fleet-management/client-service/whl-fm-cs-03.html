WHL-FM-CS-03

Client Complaint and Escalation Management

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Service & Account Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Client Complaint and Escalation Management process at Wheels ensures that all client complaints are handled efficiently, documented properly, and escalated appropriately to resolution. This process involves multiple roles and systems to address issues from initial intake to final closure.
Trigger
A client submits a complaint through the FleetView portal or directly to an Account Manager.
Outcome
The complaint is resolved satisfactorily with documentation and follow-up actions as required, minimizing impact on service quality and maintaining client satisfaction.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Timely complaint resolution within 24 hours
  • 90% of complaints escalated within 1 hour
  • 100% accurate assignment within 30 minutes
  • 95% driver notification rate within 1 hour
  • 85% accurate data analysis within 2 hours
  • 100% documentation accuracy
  • 98% of cases closed within 2 days
  • 90% client notification rate within 1 hour
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delayed complaint logging due to system issues.
  • Misassigned complaints leading to extended resolution times.
  • Inaccurate telematics data analysis affecting decision-making.
  • Non-compliance with internal escalation protocols causing delays.
  • Client dissatisfaction due to prolonged resolution periods.
