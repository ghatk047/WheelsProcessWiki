WHL-FM-CS-02

Quarterly Business Review Data Package Assembly

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Service & Account Management 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Quarterly Business Review Data Package Assembly process involves compiling and organizing relevant data from various sources to provide comprehensive insights to clients. This helps in assessing fleet performance, financial health, and compliance status.
Trigger
Quarterly business review meeting scheduled with client
Outcome
A detailed data package assembled, reviewed, and presented to the client during the quarterly business review meeting

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Fuel purchase accuracy > 98%
  • Data integration completeness > 98%
  • Custom KPI accuracy > 95%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Incomplete or inaccurate data extraction from SAP S/4HANA
  • Inconsistent vehicle performance metrics due to telematics data issues
  • Transaction data gaps from WEX Fuel Card API
  • ETL process failures leading to incomplete data integration
  • Incorrect KPI calculations affecting the accuracy of reports
  • Slow dashboard performance impacting client interaction during presentations
  • Insufficient presentation materials causing delays in meeting preparation
  • Low client engagement despite accurate data delivery
