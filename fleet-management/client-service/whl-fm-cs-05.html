WHL-FM-CS-05

Driver Helpdesk and 24x7 Support Operations

🏢 Fleet Management Services ➡ Client Service & Account Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves managing driver-related helpdesk tickets and providing 24x7 support to ensure smooth fleet operations.
Trigger
Receiving a new ticket in the FleetView Helpdesk system from drivers or internal stakeholders
Outcome
Resolving the driver issue efficiently, ensuring minimal disruption to fleet operations

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Response time within 30 minutes
  • Ticket assignment accuracy > 95%
  • Customer satisfaction score > 80%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • System downtime impacting ticket creation and resolution processes
  • Data inconsistencies leading to incorrect decisions
  • Misrouting of tickets due to improper configuration
  • Manual errors in invoice adjustments causing financial discrepancies
  • Delayed MVR updates affecting driver safety programs
