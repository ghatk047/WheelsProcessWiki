WHL-AM-SB-04

Negotiation and Settlement with Third-Party Carrier

🏢 Accident Management ➡ Subrogation & Cost Recovery 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The process for negotiating and settling claims with third-party carriers in the event of an accident involving a vehicle managed by Wheels.
Trigger
An accident report is filed in the Wheels Accident Management Platform.
Outcome
Successful negotiation and settlement with the third-party carrier, resulting in cost recovery for Wheels.
Wheels Accident Management PlatformSAP S/4HANA

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Accident reports filed within 24 hours > 90%
  • Claim notifications sent within 48 hours > 95%
  • Subrogation assessments completed within 3 days > 85%
  • Negotiations initiated within 1 day > 90%
  • Communications sent within 24 hours > 95%
  • Settlement agreements completed within 10 days > 90%
  • Payments processed within 3 days > 95%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Miscommunication between the accident management team and third-party carriers leading to extended settlement periods.
  • Errors in subrogation assessments causing missed cost recovery opportunities.
  • Delays in payment processing affecting cash flow and relationships with carriers.
