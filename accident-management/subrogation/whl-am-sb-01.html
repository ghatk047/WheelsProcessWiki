WHL-AM-SB-01

Third-Party Liability Identification at FNOL

🏢 Accident Management ➡ Subrogation & Cost Recovery 📝 10 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Third-Party Liability Identification at FNOL process identifies potential third-party liabilities during the First Notice of Loss (FNOL) process. This helps in quickly assessing and managing claims to minimize disruptions.
Trigger
Notification of an accident or incident from Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform, Wheels Accident Management Platform, or Wheels Mobile Assistant App
Outcome
Accurate identification of third-party liabilities with detailed documentation for subrogation and cost recovery efforts
