WHL-RT-RG-06

Base Plate and Cab Card Issuance Commercial

🏢 Registration & Title Management ➡ Vehicle Registration Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the issuance of base plate and cab card for commercial vehicles in Wheels' fleet. It ensures all necessary documentation is completed accurately and timely to maintain vehicle uptime and compliance.
Trigger
Receiving a request from a Fleet Consultant or Account Manager indicating a new vehicle registration is needed.
Outcome
A successfully issued base plate and cab card for the commercial vehicle, with all related documents processed and stored in the system.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Order creation time < 1 hour

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delayed order creation affecting overall registration timeline.
  • Incorrect invoice generation leading to payment delays.
  • Errors in validation leading to rework.
  • Delayed submissions affecting vehicle availability.
  • Lost or misplaced cards affecting driver's ability to operate the vehicle.
