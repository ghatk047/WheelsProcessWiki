WHL-FL-OL-07

Depreciation and Book Value Reconciliation

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Depreciation and Book Value Reconciliation process ensures accurate financial records by calculating depreciation based on leasing terms and asset values, and reconciling these with SAP S/4HANA.
Trigger
End of lease period or change in vehicle condition
Outcome
Accurate depreciation records updated in SAP S/4HANA, ensuring compliance with financial reporting standards

⚠️ Operational Risks

  • Manual input errors in depreciation calculations affecting financial records
  • Data discrepancies between FleetView and SAP S/4HANA causing reconciliation issues
  • Delayed reporting impacting client financial planning and trust
