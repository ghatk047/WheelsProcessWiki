WHL-FL-OL-02

Vehicle Capitalised Cost and Residual Value Setting

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Vehicle Capitalised Cost and Residual Value Setting process at Wheels involves calculating the initial capital cost of a vehicle and estimating its residual value at lease end. This ensures accurate financial planning and compliance with regulatory requirements.
Trigger
Initiation by a Fleet Consultant or Account Manager when a new lease agreement is proposed or reviewed, typically after a lease contract has been signed.
Outcome
Successful completion sets the capitalised cost and residual value in SAP S/4HANA for accurate accounting and financial reporting.
