WHL-FL-OL-02

Vehicle Capitalised Cost and Residual Value Setting

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process sets up the initial capitalised cost and residual value for open-end leases in the fleet management system.
Trigger
Lease agreement signing
Outcome
Accurate capitalised cost and residual value set, approved by finance team
