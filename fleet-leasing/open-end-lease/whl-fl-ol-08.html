WHL-FL-OL-08

Lease Portfolio Financial Reporting

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process ensures accurate and timely financial reporting for the open-end lease portfolio. It involves extracting data from various systems, aggregating it, and generating reports that provide insights into financial performance.
Trigger
Monthly lease account review or upon request by internal stakeholders
Outcome
A comprehensive financial report that includes lease payments, residual values, depreciation, and other relevant metrics for each open-end lease in the portfolio.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Data extraction time < 1 hour
  • Data integration completion time < 2 hours
  • Report generation time < 1 hour
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data inconsistencies between systems affecting financial reporting accuracy.
  • SAP system downtime impacting data extraction and processing times.
  • Cloud security breaches compromising sensitive report storage.
  • Email delivery failures resulting in delayed report distribution to stakeholders.
