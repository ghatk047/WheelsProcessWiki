WHL-FL-OL-03

Lease Agreement Execution and Documentation

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Lease Agreement Execution and Documentation process involves the creation, management, and finalization of lease agreements for corporate clients.
Trigger
Receipt of a new lease request from an Account Manager
Outcome
A fully executed and documented lease agreement with all necessary approvals and client signatures

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Lease agreement creation time < 3 business days
  • Client approval rate > 90%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Payment processing time < 24 hours
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data entry errors in initial lease orders causing delays
  • Client dissatisfaction due to inaccurate fuel quotes
  • Financial disputes arising from incorrect invoice data
  • Late payment processing affecting cash flow
  • Inaccurate contract updates leading to lease execution issues
