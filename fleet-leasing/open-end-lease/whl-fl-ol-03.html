WHL-FL-OL-03

Lease Agreement Execution and Documentation

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Lease Agreement Execution and Documentation process ensures that lease agreements are accurately created, reviewed, and documented for fleet leasing and financing operations.
Trigger
Initiation of a new lease request by a corporate client through the FleetView portal or direct contact with an Account Manager.
Outcome
A fully executed and documented lease agreement ready for implementation and monitoring within the Wheels fleet management system.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANA

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Order creation time < 24 hours
  • Contract drafting time < 48 hours
  • Review initiation within 2 business days
  • Approval time < 7 days
  • Finalization time < 2 days
  • Execution confirmation within 1 business day
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delays in legal review causing lease agreement delays.
  • Errors in contract drafting leading to legal issues.
  • Accounting discrepancies due to errors in finalization.
  • Delayed confirmations causing fleet deployment delays.
