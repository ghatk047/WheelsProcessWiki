WHL-FL-OL-01

Client Lease Programme Design and Structuring

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Client Lease Programme Design and Structuring process involves defining and structuring the lease terms and conditions for clients' fleet leasing programs, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and maximizing client satisfaction.
Trigger
A new client or an existing client requiring a review of their current lease program
Outcome
A structured lease program designed to meet the client's needs, including detailed terms, conditions, and associated documentation ready for implementation.
