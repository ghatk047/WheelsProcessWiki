WHL-FL-OL-06

Lease Termination and Vehicle Return Processing

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
Lease termination and vehicle return processing at Wheels involves formalizing the end of a lease agreement and ensuring the vehicle is returned in compliance with contractual terms.
Trigger
Completion of lease term or early termination request by the lessee
Outcome
Successful completion results in the vehicle being returned to Wheels, lease records are updated, and any financial adjustments are made.
