WHL-FL-OL-06

Lease Termination and Vehicle Return Processing

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Open-End Lease Management 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The process for terminating an open-end lease and processing vehicle return at Wheels involves several key steps to ensure compliance, accuracy, and timely return of the leased vehicles.
Trigger
Notification from client or lease expiration date
Outcome
Successful termination of the lease and proper handling of vehicle return with all associated records updated in SAP S/4HANA and FleetView.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Response time to client request < 48 hours
  • Accuracy of termination records > 98%
  • Completion rate of condition assessments > 95%
  • Review time for condition assessments < 24 hours
  • Resolution rate of discrepancies > 90%
  • Closure time for leases < 7 days
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Delayed processing of termination requests affecting client satisfaction
  • Incorrect updates leading to financial discrepancies
  • Incomplete or delayed inspections impacting lease closure
  • Errors or omissions in review delaying closure
  • Unresolved issues causing prolonged closure
  • Extended closure periods affecting cash flow and client relationships
