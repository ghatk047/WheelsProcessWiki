WHL-FL-CF-06

Early Termination Penalty Calculation

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Early Termination Penalty Calculation process determines the penalty amount for customers who terminate their lease agreement before its agreed term.
Trigger
Customer submits a request to terminate the lease early
Outcome
Calculation of the early termination penalty and communication of the result to the customer
