WHL-FL-CF-03

Closed-End Lease Agreement Execution

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Closed-End Lease Agreement Execution process at Wheels involves the initiation, processing, and closure of lease agreements for fleet vehicles. This ensures that all necessary documents are prepared and processed accurately to meet contractual obligations.
Trigger
A new lease agreement request is received from a client through the FleetView portal or Salesforce CRM.
Outcome
Successful execution of a closed-end lease agreement with accurate documentation, financial setup in SAP S/4HANA, and integration with various telematics and compliance systems.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWEX Fuel Card APIWheels Telematics/IoT Platform

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Lease agreements created within 2 business days
  • Financial records created within 1 business day
  • Fuel cards activated within 24 hours
  • Telematics devices activated within 1 business day
  • Invoices posted within 1 business day
  • Confirmation emails sent within 1 business day of invoice posting
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data entry errors leading to delays in lease agreement creation.
  • Integration issues between FleetView and SAP causing financial setup delays.
  • API connection issues with WEX Fuel Card API affecting fuel card account setups.
  • Device connectivity issues with Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform delaying telematics activations.
  • Manual intervention required for complex lease terms leading to delays in invoice posting.
