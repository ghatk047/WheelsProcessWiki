WHL-FL-CF-04

Fleet Financing Loan Origination and Funding

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
Fleet Financing Loan Origination and Funding is the process of initiating a loan application for fleet leasing and financing, processing the required documentation, and funding the approved loans.
Trigger
A new lease or financing request is received from a corporate client through FleetView.
Outcome
Successful completion results in the origination and funding of a fleet financing loan, ensuring that the necessary financial resources are allocated to the client's vehicle leasing needs.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWEX Fuel Card API

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Application accuracy > 98%
  • Credit approval rate > 90%
  • Fraud detection rate > 95%
  • Report generation time < 1 hour
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Payment processing time < 2 hours
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Incomplete or inaccurate lease/financing applications.
  • Delays in credit validation processes.
  • False positives in fraud detection systems.
  • Errors in financial analysis report generation.
  • Financial discrepancies due to invoice errors.
