WHL-FL-CF-02

Vehicle Residual Guarantee Agreement Management

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 6 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Vehicle Residual Guarantee Agreement Management process at Wheels involves managing agreements that ensure the residual value of leased vehicles meets predefined standards, impacting lease returns and financial reporting.
Trigger
Initiation by the Account Manager when a new closed-end lease agreement is signed or modified
Outcome
A successful completion results in accurate valuation of the vehicle residual value, ensuring compliance with leasing agreements and minimizing financial risks.
