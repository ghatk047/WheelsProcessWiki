WHL-FL-CF-02

Vehicle Residual Guarantee Agreement Management

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the Vehicle Residual Guarantee Agreement for closed-end leases and financings. It ensures that all residual values are accurately assessed and reported, facilitating fair financial settlements between Wheels and its clients.
Trigger
A lease agreement nearing its end term
Outcome
Accurate residual value assessment and final settlement of the lease agreement with the client

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Incomplete lease data affecting residual calculations

  • Incorrect posting causing financial errors
  • Unverified transactions affecting residual values
  • Inaccurate documents causing client disputes
