WHL-FL-CF-05

Monthly Billing and Payment Processing

🏢 Fleet Leasing & Financing ➡ Closed-End Lease and Financing 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
Monthly billing and payment processing for closed-end leases at Wheels involves generating accurate invoices based on lease terms, vehicle usage data, and fuel card transactions, followed by secure payment collection and reconciliation.
Trigger
End of the billing cycle (e.g., last day of the month)
Outcome
Accurate and timely invoicing, successful payment processing, and reconciled records in SAP S/4HANA.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWEX Fuel Card API
📈 BPMN Process Flow (Technology-Focused)

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Transaction accuracy > 98%
  • Invoice review time < 2 days
  • Payment collection time < 10 days
  • Reconciliation accuracy > 98%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate lease terms leading to erroneous billing.
  • Data inconsistencies causing delays in invoice generation.
  • Fraudulent transactions affecting financial security.
  • System errors during bulk invoicing impacting cash flow.
  • Manual reconciliation prone to human error.
