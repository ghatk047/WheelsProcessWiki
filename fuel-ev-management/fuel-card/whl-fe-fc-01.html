WHL-FE-FC-01

WEX Fuel Card Issuance and Driver Assignment

🏢 Fuel & EV Charging Management ➡ Fuel Card Programme Management 📝 7 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process manages the issuance and assignment of WEX fuel cards to drivers within Wheels' fleet. It ensures that drivers have access to necessary fuel card services while maintaining compliance and security.
Trigger
A new driver is onboarded or an existing driver's fuel card needs renewal/assignment.
Outcome
Drivers are assigned active WEX fuel cards, with all relevant data updated in the system for tracking and compliance.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWEX Fuel Card API

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data discrepancies between FleetView and SAP S/4HANA leading to incorrect fuel card assignments.
  • Delays in WEX API response affecting driver availability.
  • Incorrect invoices posted in SAP S/4HANA causing financial issues.
  • Drivers not receiving push notifications from the FleetView Mobile App leading to confusion.
  • Security breaches of sensitive data during fuel card issuance.
