WHL-MT-PM-08

Maintenance Cost Trend Analysis and Reporting

🏢 Maintenance Management ➡ Preventive Maintenance Management 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
This process involves analyzing maintenance cost trends and generating reports to optimize fleet management and reduce costs. It tracks preventive maintenance activities, identifies cost patterns, and provides actionable insights.
Trigger
Scheduled monthly at the end of each billing cycle
Outcome
Provision of detailed maintenance cost trend analysis reports and visualizations for informed decision-making by the Fleet Management team.

⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Data extraction errors leading to incorrect analysis.
  • Slow report generation affecting decision-making timelines.
  • Delayed updates to the maintenance schedule causing missed windows.
  • Inaccurate procurement plans resulting in stock management issues.
