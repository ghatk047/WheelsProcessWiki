WHL-MT-PM-04

Work Order Creation and Repair Authorisation

🏢 Maintenance Management ➡ Preventive Maintenance Management 📝 9 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Work Order Creation and Repair Authorisation process ensures that maintenance tasks are scheduled, authorised, and tracked efficiently to maintain vehicle uptime and ensure compliance with safety standards.
Trigger
Vehicle health monitoring data indicates a preventive maintenance need or an anomaly detected through the Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform.
Outcome
A work order is created, approved, executed, and closed, with all repair authorisations recorded for auditing and compliance purposes.

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Work order creation time < 24 hours
  • Approval time < 2 hours
  • Repair scheduling accuracy > 98%
  • Inventory accuracy > 95%
  • Vehicle uptime > 95%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inaccurate work order data leading to missed maintenance
  • Delayed approvals causing vehicle downtime
  • Incorrect inventory levels affecting repair timelines
  • Late reimbursement payments affecting driver morale
  • Manual closure processes increasing administrative burden
