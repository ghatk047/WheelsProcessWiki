WHL-MT-UR-02

Roadside Assistance Dispatch and Tow Coordination

🏢 Maintenance Management ➡ Unscheduled Repair & Roadside Management 📝 8 L4 steps ✅ Complete
Description
The Roadside Assistance Dispatch and Tow Coordination process at Wheels involves the seamless dispatch of roadside assistance providers to stranded vehicles and coordination of towing services, ensuring minimal downtime and efficient resolution.
Trigger
Vehicle Breakdown Alert from Wheels Telematics/IoT Platform
Outcome
Successfully dispatched roadside assistance and towed vehicle to a repair location with minimal delay and accurate billing.
FleetViewSAP S/4HANAWheels Telematics/IoT PlatformWEX Fuel Card API

🎯 KPIs & Performance Targets
  • Response time within 5 minutes
  • Ticket creation within 1 minute of alert
  • Dispatch time within 2 minutes of incident log
  • Incident closure rate > 98%
  • Invoice accuracy > 99%
  • Payment settlement within 24 hours
⚠️ Operational Risks
  • Inadequate provider availability causing delays in dispatch
  • Provider response variability affecting on-time arrival
  • Inaccurate tow request data leading to inefficient service delivery
  • Incomplete or inaccurate resolution data affecting analytics and reporting
  • Manual intervention required for billing, increasing processing time
